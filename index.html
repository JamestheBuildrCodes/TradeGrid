FAQ

Questions. Answered.

What brokers does TradeGrid support?
TradeGrid supports MT4, MT5, cTrader, OANDA, and all major prop firm CSV exports (FTMO, My Forex Funds, True Forex Funds, E8 Funding). You can also import any broker history using our Universal CSV format. We're adding new broker APIs monthly — check our supported brokers page for the full list.
How does the AI coach actually work?
The AI coach uses Claude (by Anthropic) as its base model, with your entire trade history embedded into a vector database (Pinecone). When you ask a question, the AI retrieves relevant trades from your history and gives you a specific, data-backed answer — not generic advice. The more you trade, the smarter it gets. This architecture is unique to TradeGrid.
Is my trading data private and secure?
Yes. Your trade data is encrypted at rest and in transit. We use read-only API connections for broker sync — we cannot place or modify trades. Your data is never sold to third parties. The Community Edge Report uses fully anonymised, aggregated data only. You can export and delete all your data at any time.
Can I use TradeGrid for prop firm challenges?
Absolutely — prop firm traders are our core audience. TradeGrid has a dedicated Prop Firm Mode for FTMO, My Forex Funds, True Forex Funds, The Funded Trader, and E8 Funding. It tracks your daily drawdown, max drawdown, and profit target in real time, and alerts you before you breach any rules. Available on Pro and above.
What's included in the free plan?
The free plan includes 10 trades per month, manual entry, basic R:R analytics, and the performance calendar. It's designed to let you experience the core journal and analytics before upgrading. No credit card required, and you can upgrade anytime.
What is the Founder's Lifetime Access deal?

Founders get everything in Elite forever, founding member badge, direct input on the product roadmap, first access to new features, and a private community. There's a 14-day money-back guarantee.
